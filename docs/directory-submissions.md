### 6. Product Hunt (DO NOT submit-and-forget)

- **Submit URL:** https://www.producthunt.com/posts/new
- **Account needed:** Free Product Hunt account
- **Why this is different:** Product Hunt is a launch platform, not a directory. A submission gets **24 hours of upvote voting** then settles. The value is in the launch-day traffic and "Product of the Day" badge, NOT in the long-tail directory listing.

**Recommended approach:**

1. **Now:** Create your Product Hunt account so you're ready.
2. **Pick a launch date** — Tuesday/Wednesday morning Pacific time gives the best vote-up window. Pick one ~2 weeks out so you have time to prep.
3. **Pre-launch (week before):**
   - Email your Defyn Digital newsletter (if any)
   - Post on LinkedIn announcing "we're launching on Product Hunt next Tuesday"
   - Ask 5-10 friendly Shopify devs in your network to be early upvoters
   - Have a "ship" post ready for your Defyn blog
4. **Launch day:**
   - Post at 12:01 AM Pacific time (gives 24h to accumulate votes)
   - Reply to every comment that day
   - Cross-post to Twitter, LinkedIn, Indie Hackers, /r/shopify
5. **Post-launch:** The Product Hunt listing stays forever and gets indexed by AI search.

**Fields for the launch post:**
- Tagline → use 60-char tagline above
- Description → use the 300-char one
- Gallery → 4-6 screenshots + a 30-60s loom video
- Topics → `SaaS`, `E-commerce`, `Shopify`, `Web Performance`, `Developer Tools`
- Pricing → Freemium

I can drive the launch day for you via Chrome MCP when you're ready — just say "launch Product Hunt today" and we go.
